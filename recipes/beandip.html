Bean Dip Recipe

A simple and easy recipe for a yummy vegan bean dip.

The veg-head in me is annoyed that I must clarify that a dip made of beans is vegan. But one can never underestimate omnis from adding animal products into everything.

  • Two cans of refried beans
  • One can of black beans
  • One can of salsa
  • One can of black olives
  • Olive oil, lime, salt, pepper, cumin, oregano onion powder, and garlic powder to taste

Steps

  1. Add beans and salsa into a bowl
  2. Slice olives and add
  3. Combine the rest of the ingredients and wa la!
