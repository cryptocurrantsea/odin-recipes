Salad

My favorite way to make salad

Two main ingredients in this bad boy: artichokes and heart of palm

Ingredients

  • One head of lettuce
  • Lots of hemp hearts
  • 5 quartered artichoke hearts
  • 1-2 cylinders of heart of palm
  • Small handful of kalamata olives
  • Spoonful of sauerkraut
  • 1 can of kidney beans
  • Olive oil, ACV, salt, and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Chop up the lettuce smaller than you would normally see in a salad. This is so you can eat it with a spoon, like a civilized person.
  2. Cut up artichoke hearts and heart of palm into bite sized pieces
  3. Slice kalamata olives into fours
  4. Combine all of the ingredients together
